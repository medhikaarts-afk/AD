📚 Help Center & FAQs

📱 How do I configure WhatsApp campaigns? ▼
Switch to the Marketing module, scan the connection QR code using your mobile phone, select your client audience segment (e.g. VIP, Inactive), compose your draft with customized tokens (like {name}), and launch! Background queues will dispatch messages safely with simulated delay.
💇 How are Stylist commissions computed? ▼
Stylist commissions are automatically calculated as a percentage of total completed booking invoice totals that they styled. You can configure individual commission rates and track payout logs directly inside the Team Management tab.
💵 How do I record daily business expenses? ▼
Navigate to the Expense Log, click "+ Record Expense", enter description, amount, select category, and tap submit. The main dashboard updates immediately and subtracts expenses to display your net operating income!
⚙️ What does "LocalStorage Mode" mean? ▼
The application operates offline-first, preserving all booking records, stylists, expenses, and settings within the browser session caches. This ensures zero-downtime, maximum speed, and lets you manage the salon locally even if active database servers are down.
